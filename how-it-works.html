How Blockchain Voting Works

A transparent, secure, and verifiable voting process

The Voting Process

1

Voter Authentication

Verify your identity using secure government-issued credentials through our multi-factor authentication system.

2

Ballot Access

Receive your unique, encrypted ballot tailored to your electoral district and eligible races.

3

Make Your Selection

Review candidate information and select your choices on the digital ballot.

4

Encrypt & Submit

Your selections are encrypted and submitted to the blockchain network for verification.

5

Blockchain Verification

Network nodes verify and record your vote as an immutable transaction on the blockchain.

6

Receipt Generation

Receive a unique verification code to confirm your vote was recorded without revealing your selection.

The Technology Behind E-Vótáil

Hyperledger Fabric

Enterprise-grade blockchain framework providing modular architecture for voting transactions.

Zero-Knowledge Proofs

Advanced cryptography that verifies votes without revealing voter identity or selections.

Multi-Factor Authentication

Combination of PPSN, mobile verification, and biometrics for secure access.

Verifiable Audit Trail

Each voter receives a unique QR code to confirm their vote was counted in the final tally.
