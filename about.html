About E-Vótáil

Revolutionizing democracy through blockchain technology

Our Mission

E-Vótáil was created to enhance democratic participation in Ireland by providing a secure, transparent, and accessible online voting platform. We combine cutting-edge blockchain technology with rigorous security protocols to deliver a voting system you can trust.

Our goal is to increase voter turnout, reduce election costs, and provide verifiable results while maintaining the secrecy and integrity of every ballot.
